About

Ideas for brands. Soundtracks for culture.

I’m Raka Ashok. A multidisciplinary creative, brand designer, music producer, DJ and curator working at the intersection of culture, design and sound. For over a decade, I’ve helped ambitious brands find their voice while building communities, events and experiences that move people physically and emotionally. From identity systems to dancefloors, my work is driven by one question: How does it feel?

Culture First. Medium Agnostic.

I don’t believe creativity lives in silos. The same curiosity that shapes a brand identity influences a DJ set. The same instinct that builds a community can shape a visual system.

For over 13 years, I’ve operated at the intersection of art / design, culture and sound - building brands, shaping experiences and creating moments that people don’t just notice, but feel.

My approach combines strategic thinking with artistic intuition. Less trend chasing. More culture building.
