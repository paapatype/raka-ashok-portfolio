About

Raka Ashok works the seam where art meets bass — then designs the poster for it.

A producer, DJ and visual artist from Bangalore, Raka builds his sets and records out of heavy drums, tight snares and unconventional sampling — threading UK garage, breaks, drum & bass and hip-hop through sounds picked up around South India.

When he isn’t behind the decks — or trading ideas on bass-heavy experiments like RAAJA BEATS with MALFNKTION — he’s building brands, identities and gig posters for the same scene he plays in. One discipline feeds the other; it’s all the same pair of hands.
